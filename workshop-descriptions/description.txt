[h1]Phobos' Chemistry Pathways[/h1]
A complete chemistry crafting suite for Project Zomboid Build 42. Turn raw materials into gunpowder, biodiesel fuel, soap, and more through realistic multi-step processes.

[b]150 recipes[/b] across 7 crafting pathways, a custom [b]Applied Chemistry[/b] skill, 2 occupations, 2 traits, and [b]12 sandbox options[/b] for full server control.

[hr][/hr]
[h2]Recipe Pathways[/h2]
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/phobos-dthorga/mod-pz-chemistry-pathways/main/docs/images/recipe-overview.png[/img]
[i]Full recipe overview — [url=https://github.com/phobos-dthorga/mod-pz-chemistry-pathways/tree/main/docs]view all diagrams on GitHub[/url][/i]

[list]
[*][b]Blackpowder Synthesis[/b] — 7-step chain: crush charcoal, purify, extract sulphur from battery acid, synthesize KNO3 from compost, mix gunpowder
[*][b]Biodiesel Production[/b] — 5-step chain: press oils, render fat, distill methanol, transesterify, wash and refine into 5L petrol cans
[*][b]Soap-Making[/b] — Glycerol soap from biodiesel by-products, fat-based soap from rendered fat + KOH
[*][b]Bone Char[/b] — Pyrolyse animal bones into bone char (substitutes for purified charcoal)
[*][b]Recycling[/b] — 8 recipes: wood tar to glue, calcite to quicklime/fertilizer, soap to bandages, lead to fishing tackle, plastics to glue, acid-wash electronics for transistors
[*][b]Advanced Lab[/b] — Centrifuge and chromatograph recipes for higher purity yields (requires zReVaccin 3 lab equipment)
[*][b]Fat Rendering & Oil Conversion[/b] — Convert cooking oils and animal fats into feedstock
[/list]

[h2]Skill System[/h2]
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/phobos-dthorga/mod-pz-chemistry-pathways/main/docs/images/skill-progression.png[/img]

[b]Applied Chemistry (AC)[/b] — custom skill from level 0 to 10. Recipes unlock as you level up. Higher tiers require the Chemistry Pathways Handbook (lootable).

[list]
[*][b]Chemist[/b] occupation — starts at AC 3, 13 pre-learned recipes
[*][b]Pharmacist[/b] occupation — starts at AC 2, 5 pre-learned recipes
[*][b]Chemistry Enthusiast[/b] trait — +1 AC, 2 bonus recipes (+4 points)
[*][b]Chemical Aversion[/b] trait — -1 AC (-2 points)
[*]5 skill books (Vol 1-5) distributed across bookshelves
[/list]

[hr][/hr]
[h2]Sandbox Options (12)[/h2]
Full control for server admins and singleplayer alike:
[list]
[*]Enable/disable health hazards (PPE system with safe/unsafe recipe variants)
[*]Enable/disable advanced lab equipment recipes
[*]Toggle heat source requirements
[*]Enable/disable impurity tracking system with 3 severity levels
[*]Yield multiplier (0.25x to 4.0x)
[*]Purity feedback display
[*]5 tiered reset options (strip purity data, forget recipes, reset XP, remove items, nuclear reset)
[/list]
[i][url=https://github.com/phobos-dthorga/mod-pz-chemistry-pathways/tree/main/docs]Full sandbox documentation on GitHub[/url][/i]

[hr][/hr]
[h2]Cross-Mod Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]EHR (Extensive Health Rework)[/b] — If installed, hazardous recipes dispatch EHR diseases instead of vanilla stat penalties. Auto-detected, no config needed.
[*][b]ZScienceSkill[/b] — If installed, mirrors 50% of Applied Chemistry XP to Science skill + registers 33 items and 8 fluids as specimens. Auto-detected.
[/list]
No errors if these mods are absent — everything degrades gracefully to vanilla behaviour.
